About the platform

ESF-8 University is being modernized into a clean, accessible platform for public health emergency preparedness, medical response education, and collaborative situational awareness.

✚

Mission

Make emergency response information easier to find, understand, and use before, during, and after public health incidents.

▣

Audience

Students, faculty, public health professionals, healthcare teams, and partner organizations involved in preparedness and response.

↗

Focus

Training resources, VMOC integration, disaster readiness tools, and coordinated response support across institutions.

ESF-8 Reference

Explore the 17 core ESF-8 functions.

ESF-8 covers the public health and medical capabilities needed to assess needs, coordinate care, move patients, support medical surge, protect critical supplies, and manage health consequences during emergencies.

View All Functions Preparedness Areas
Assessment Surveillance Medical Response Safety Environmental Health
Assessment & surveillance Identify needs, monitor health conditions, and provide timely public health information.
Medical response Support surge, patient movement, patient care, behavioral health, and medical supplies.
Safety & environment Protect food, water, drugs, biologics, medical devices, agriculture, and vector control systems.
Consequence management Support mass fatality planning, victim identification, and health hazard mitigation.
