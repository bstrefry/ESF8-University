About the platform

ESF-8 University is being modernized into a clean, accessible platform for public health emergency preparedness, medical response education, and collaborative situational awareness.

Mission

Support health and medical preparedness by making emergency response information easier to access, understand, and use.

Audience

Students, faculty, public health professionals, healthcare teams, and partner organizations involved in preparedness and response.

Focus

Training resources, VMOC integration, disaster readiness tools, and coordinated response support across institutions.
