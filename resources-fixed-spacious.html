Workflow

How to use the materials

The page works best as a simple planning sequence: define the structure, set up coordination, prepare the tools, then train the team.

1

Start with the emergency operations framework.

Use the planning templates to clarify roles, response priorities, and coordination pathways.

2

Build the VMOC reporting structure.

Use reporting templates and situation report guidance to support regular situational awareness.

3

Prepare staffing and resource tools.

Use operational templates to support personnel planning, allocation, and communication.

4

Train, test, and revise.

Use checklists, slides, and exercises to make the plan practical before it is needed.
