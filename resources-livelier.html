How to use this page

Turn links into action.

During an incident, resources are most useful when they support a decision. Use this page to identify the situation, check official sources, monitor changes, and connect findings to VMOC updates or planning documents.

1. Identify the hazardStart with weather, disaster, or public health monitoring sources.
2. Confirm with official guidanceUse CDC, FEMA, ASPR, WHO, or NWS as primary reference points.
3. Connect to operationsTranslate guidance into preparedness actions, training, or coordination needs.
4. Update situational awarenessUse VMOC updates and monitoring tools to track how the situation changes.
